What is Oldies

Oldies is a streaming service dedicated to bringing you the best classic movies from the golden age of cinema. Our extensive library features films from the 1920s to the 1970s, including iconic titles such as Casablanca, Gone with the Wind, and The Godfather.

We believe that classic movies should be easily accessible to everyone, which is why we offer a simple and user-friendly platform for you to enjoy your favorite films. Whether you're a long-time fan or new to classic cinema, our collection has something for everyone.

Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the rich history of cinema by providing a platform for these timeless classics. We are passionate about bringing these films to a new generation of viewers and hope to inspire a renewed love for the art of storytelling.

Features

Here's what you can expect when you join our platform:

  • Extensive Library: Our collection includes a wide range of classic movies from different genres, eras, and countries. From Hollywood blockbusters to foreign films, there's something for everyone on Oldies.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Our recommendation engine uses advanced algorithms to suggest movies based on your viewing history, preferences, and ratings. This makes it easy for you to discover new films and enjoy old favorites.
  • User-friendly Interface: We have designed our platform to be intuitive and easy to use, with a clean and simple interface that allows you to navigate and search for movies with ease.
  • High-quality Streaming: We offer high-quality streaming of classic movies, with options for different resolutions and playback speeds.
  • Offline Viewing: With our offline viewing feature, you can download movies to your device and watch them later, even when you don't have an internet connection.
  • Multi-Device Support: You can access Oldies on multiple devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and smart TVs, making it easy for you to watch your favorite classic movies anytime, anywhere.

We are constantly working to improve and add new features to our platform, with the goal of providing the best possible streaming experience for classic movie lovers.

Pricing

Oldies offers affordable pricing plans that cater to different needs, so whether you're a casual viewer or a die-hard fan of classic cinema, we've got you covered. We believe that classic movies should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget.

This plan is ideal for families or groups who want to share their love of classic movies with each other.

All our plans come with a 7-day free trial, so you can try our platform and see if it's the right fit for you. We also offer a range of payment options, including credit card, PayPal, and more.
